Welcome to my new personal website! I've been meaning to set up a space where I can share my thoughts, projects, and experiences as a software engineer.

## Why a Personal Website?

Having your own website is like having your own little corner of the internet. It's a place to:

- **Share knowledge** — Writing about what I learn helps me solidify my understanding
- **Document my journey** — A record of growth and learning over time
- **Connect with others** — A way for people to find and reach out to me

## The Tech Stack

This site is built with:

- **Next.js** with the App Router for a modern React experience
- **Tailwind CSS** for utility-first styling
- **MDX** for writing blog posts with embedded components
- **next-themes** for dark/light mode support

## What's Next?

I plan to write about software engineering, web development, and the interesting problems I encounter along the way. Stay tuned!
